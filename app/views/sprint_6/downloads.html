Downloads

On this page you can download GGiS data from your organisation and the rest of government. The spreadsheets are generated for you using live GGiS data.

You can also filter and download spreadsheets on the following pages:

  • View grant schemes
  • View your awards

You can search for a recipient then download all their awards through the following page:

  • View recipients
Read this before using this data

Accuracy of GGiS data is the responsibility of the funding departments.

Data downloaded from GGiS should only be utilised internally and should not be used to make public facing statistical claims unless express agreement from the department's Grants Champion has been sought.

For publicly available grants data, please refer to the published Government grants data and statistics.

My Organisation’s Schemes

For Arm’s-length body (ALB) users, this will include all schemes managed by your ALB.

For central department users, this will include all schemes managed by either your department or an ALB your department sponsors.

Includes all years for which GGiS has data.

My Organisation’s Awards

For ALB users, this will include all awards managed by your ALB.

For central department users, this will include all awards mananged by either your department or an ALB your department sponsors.

Includes all years for which GGiS has data.

My Organisation’s Grants in Aid

Grants in aid are a way for one part of the government to fund another.

Includes all years for which GGiS has data.

This is only visible to central departments.

All Schemes

Includes all years for which GGiS has data, and schemes for all government departments and ALBs.

You can view data from all departments unless it has been marked as “do not publish”.

All Departments’ Awards

Includes data for the current and past two financial years. Historical data can be found on the page for Government grants data and statistics.

You can view data from all departments unless it has been marked as “do not publish”.

User Licences

Lists all active users in your department and what type of GGiS account they have.

This is only visible to GGiS Grants Champions.

All Departments’ GGiS Grants Champions

This is only visible to GGiS Grants Champions.

Downloads

Here are some of the spreadsheets you are able to download.

You can filter and download spreadsheets on the following pages:

  • View schemes
  • View awards
  • Recipient records

My Organisation’s Schemes

Download a spreadsheet listing your organisation’s grant schemes.

If your organisation sponsors ALBs, their awards will be included

My Organisation’s Awards

Download a spreadsheet listing your organisation’s grant awards.

If your organisation sponsors ALBs, their awards will be included

My Organisation’s Grants in Aid

Download a spreadsheet listing your organisation’s grants in aid.

All Schemes

Download a spreadsheet listing all grant schemes on GGiS.

You can view all data from other departments unless it has been marked as “do not publish”.

All Departments’ Awards

Download a spreadsheet listing all grant awards on GGiS.

You can view all data from other departments unless it has been marked as “do not publish”.

User Licences

Download a spreadsheet listing all active users in your department and what type of GGiS account they have.

This is only visible to GGIS Grants Champions.

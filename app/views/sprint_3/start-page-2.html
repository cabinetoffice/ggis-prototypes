Home

Welcome to GGiS, the Government Grants Information System.

Here you can explore grants data for your organisation and the rest of government. Users with edit access can upload and edit their grants data.

Grant Schemes

Grant schemes are programmes for making one or more grant award in order to contribute to a policy objective.

Here you can view, create and edit grant schemes and grants in aid, which are transfers from one part of government to another.

  • Add a grant scheme
  • View grant schemes
  • Add a grant in aid
  • View grants in aid

Grant Awards

Grant awards are paid by a grant maker to a recipient. Every award comes under a parent grant scheme.

Here you can view, create and edit awards, and view recipients.

  • Upload and edit awards in bulk
  • Previous uploads
  • Add an award
  • View your awards
  • View recipients

Downloads

Here you can download spreadsheets with data about grant schemes, grant awards and grants in aid.

Grants champions can also download spreadsheets with GGIS user data.
